SmartBuy is an indian e-commerce mobile selling company,based in Hyderabad, India The company initially focuseing on online book sales before expanding into other product categories

The service competes primarily with Amazon's Indian subsidiary and domestic rival Snapdeal. As of March 2017, SmartBuy held a 39.5% market share of India's e-commerce industry.SmartBuy has a dominant position in the apparel segment and was described as being "neck and neck" with Amazon in the sale of mobile phones. SmartBuy also owns PhonePe, a mobile payments service based on the Unified Payments Interface.
